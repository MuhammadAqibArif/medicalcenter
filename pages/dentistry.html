Dentistry Services

Veneers ( Hollywood Smile )

Veneers ( Hollywood smile ) Veneers are thin, custom-made shells of tooth-colored materials designed to cover the front surface of teeth to improve your appearance. These shells are bonded to the front of the teeth changing their color, shape, size, or length. Veneers have high resistance to discoloration, unlike bleaching. It only takes two visits to the dentist for changing your smile.

Crown And Bridge

The main functional lies in replacing missing teeth, protecting weak ones, restoring the broken tooth, and achieve a beautiful smile. In Dr. Shaikha Al-kubaisi Medical Center we use the strongest material and more stable in color for making the prosthesis. the doctor can prepare and fix the crowns & bridges in two visits only “

Routine Dental Checkup

Cleaning teeth is the best prevention from all dental problems. Extra to the daily brushing patients can visit their dentist for a professional cleaning ( scaling ) where the doctor will be able to remove the calculus and hard deposits between teeth. Scaling and polishing can be done every 6 months.

Pedodontic

Pedodontic includes all type of dental treatment for deciduous teeth like filling, pupotpmy , pulpectomy ,extraction, space maintenance, fluoride application, and protecting permanent teeth by ( fissures sealant)

Teeth Bleaching

Bleaching is a common and safe procedure in dental clinics, It is making teeth whiter than it is. Dentist uses special material for bleaching the teeth and protecting the soft tissue. The best result can be achieved by doing in-office bleaching and then continuing with home bleaching trays.

Clear Aligners

It is a clear tray aligner that is custom-made for each patient. It is a comfortable alternative to braces. The patient will change the tray every 2 weeks in the dental clinic.

Night Guard

It is a removable appliance for protecting teeth and the joint from parafunctional habits at night like clenching and grinding.

Root Canal Treatment

This is the procedure of removing the inflamed pulp and cleaning the root canals of the tooth under local anaesthesia and witout pain . The dentist will fill up the root canals after cleaning it to protect it from infection. It is better to crown the tooth and root canal treatment to protect from fractures and avoid extracting it in the future.
